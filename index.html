The Apartment

The self contained apartment is furnished in traditional Andalucian style with and sleeps up to four people comprising of a master bedroom with ensuite and a twin room also ensuite.  
The ensuites are both shower rooms and contain complementary Molton Brown products for your convenience and added enjoyment.

The apartment is fully furnished with all the modern conveniences to ensure your stay is a comfortable and enjoyable.

Sleeps maximum 4 persons

There is a further annex available which can sleep a further two people at an additional cost. The annex is not let seperately from the apartment as the privacy of our guests is important to us.

Garden design in a contemporary Moorish style is an important element of Finca fenix. Brilliant and mysterious garden design in Moorish style shows unique elegance and superb balance between design and form, creating stunning outdoor living spaces with strong visual impact cleverly captured by Finca Fenix.

Finca Fenix develops beautiful landscaping ideas in it's garden design in Hispano-Moorish style. Boasting a comprehensive  global collection of rare and unusual plants give great inspirations for creating Moorish motifs across the many terraces, adding unique character to outdoor living spaces and creating strong aesthetic appeal reflecting cultural differences and traditions in a reflective and tranquil space.
